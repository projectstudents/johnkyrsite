About Web Development

Web development refers to the creation and maintenance of websites. It involves a variety of skills such as front-end development (HTML, CSS), back-end development (server-side scripting, database management), and web design. With the growth of technology and the increasing importance of online presence, web development has become a highly sought after career path. Whether you want to create dynamic websites for businesses or build your own personal website, the opportunities for web developers are endless.
