What being a Web Developer means?

A web developer is a professional who specializes in building and maintaining websites. They use various programming languages, such as HTML, CSS, and others, to bring to life the designs and functionalities envisioned by the website owners or designers. Web developers are responsible for ensuring that websites are accessible, user-friendly, and responsive to different devices. They also test and troubleshoot websites to ensure optimal performance and security. With the increasing reliance on technology and the internet, web development is a dynamic and growing field that offers a range of opportunities for creative and tech-savvy individuals.
